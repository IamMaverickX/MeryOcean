The Goddess's Mission

Meria's journey to reconnect humanity with the ocean

In a world drifting away from nature, Meria emerges as a guide, educator, and protector of marine knowledge. Her mission is simple: to teach people about the wonders of the ocean while building a community that supports exploration, research, and discovery.

By assisting Meria in her ocean research, holders become part of her journey across the seas, from the deepest waters to the frozen frontiers of Antarctica. Each phase of the project represents a new chapter in her exploration, blending storytelling, community engagement, and decentralized participation.

Meria is not just a token, but a living narrative driven by the community, where curiosity, learning, and collective growth shape the future of the project.

Awakening of the Sea Goddess

Meria has awakened from the depths to guide humanity back to harmony with the ocean. As the Goddess of the Sea, she represents both the mystery and power of the marine world, and our responsibility to understand and protect it.

Through this project, we embark on a symbolic voyage with Meria, exploring not just financial waters but the very essence of what connects us to Earth's final frontier - the ocean.
